# SIEHS Agentic AI Training

Welcome to the **Agentic AI for Leaders** training program designed specifically for SIEHS (Sindh Integrated Emergency & Health Services) leadership and staff.

## Training Overview

This 3-day intensive program equips healthcare leaders with practical AI skills:

| Day | Focus | Key Deliverable |
|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Day 1** | Prompt Engineering | Personal Prompt Library |
| **Day 2** | n8n Workflow Automation | Working Email Automation |
| **Day 3** | AI Deployment & SDD | Live Deployed Chatbot |

## Who Should Attend

- Healthcare leaders and administrators
- Emergency services managers (1122)
- Telemedicine coordinators (Tele Tabeeb 1123)
- IT and digital transformation teams
- Operations and administrative staff

## Prerequisites

- Basic computer literacy
- Access to a laptop with internet
- Google account (for Sheets/Gmail integration)
- Willingness to learn and experiment

## About SIEHS

**Sindh Integrated Emergency & Health Services (SIEHS)** provides critical healthcare and emergency services to the Sindh province of Pakistan:

- **1122 Emergency**: 24/7 emergency response
- **Tele Tabeeb 1123**: Telemedicine services
- **RDE Training**: Rescue and disaster emergency training

This training program is designed to help SIEHS staff leverage AI to improve emergency response times, patient communication, and operational efficiency.
